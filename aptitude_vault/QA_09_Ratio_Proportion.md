## 🧠 Concept

**Ratio** compares two quantities: A:B = A/B
**Proportion** says two ratios are equal: A:B = C:D → A×D = B×C (cross multiply)

---

## 📌 Key Formulas

### Combining Ratios
If A:B = 2:3 and B:C = 4:5 → A:B:C?
> Make B same: A:B = 8:12, B:C = 12:15 → **A:B:C = 8:12:15**

### Divide in Ratio
Divide N in ratio a:b:c → Parts = N×a/(a+b+c), N×b/(a+b+c), N×c/(a+b+c)

### When ratio changes after addition/removal
> "Ratio is 3:4. After adding 5 to first, ratio becomes 4:5. Find numbers."
- 3k and 4k; (3k+5)/4k = 4/5; 15k+25=16k; k=25; **75 and 100**

### Direct Proportion: y ∝ x → y = kx
### Inverse Proportion: y ∝ 1/x → xy = k

## 🧩 Practice Questions

### Easy
1. A:B = 2:3, A = ₹40. B? → 3/2 × 40 = **₹60**
2. Divide ₹100 in 3:2 → **₹60 and ₹40**
3. A:B = 3:4, B:C = 2:5. A:C? → A:B:C = 6:8:20 = **3:10**

### Medium
4. Ratio 3:4. A gives B ₹20, becomes 1:2. Original?
   > 3k−20)/（4k+20)=1/2; 6k−40=4k+20; 2k=60; k=30; **₹90 and ₹120**
5. Milk:water = 3:2. Add 10L water, becomes 2:3. Original?
   > 3k/(2k+10)=2/3; 9k=4k+20; 5k=20; k=4; **12L and 8L**

### Hard
6. A:B:C=2:3:5. A gives ₹50 to B. New ratio: 1:4:5. Find original amounts.
   > 2k−50, 3k+50, 5k in ratio 1:4:5; (2k-50)/(3k+50)=1/4; 8k-200=3k+50; 5k=250; k=50; **100, 150, 250**

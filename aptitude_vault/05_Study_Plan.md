## 🗓️ WEEK 1: Foundations (Days 1–7)
*Goal: Build strong base in high-frequency topics*

- Read: [[QA_15_Number_System]] (full note, 45 min)

### Day 2 — Percentage
- Read: [[QA_13_Percentage]] (full note, 45 min)

- **Target:** 8/10 correct

### Day 3 — Profit & Loss
- Read: [[QA_07_Profit_Loss]] (full note, 60 min)
- Connect to [[QA_13_Percentage]] — see how % logic applies
- Practice: Problem Bank Q48–59
- Focus: Markup-discount type and same-SP type

### Day 4 — Average
- Read: [[QA_01_Average]] (full note, 45 min)
- Practice: Problem Bank Q1–15

- **Target:** 11/15 correct

### Day 5 — Ratio & Proportion
- Read: [[QA_09_Ratio_Proportion]] (full note, 45 min)

- **Target:** 7/8 correct in medium questions

### Day 6 — Ages
- Read: [[QA_06_Ages]] (full note, 30 min)
- Practice 10 age problems from topic file

### Day 7 — Week 1 Revision + Mini Mock
- Revise [[03_Formula_Sheet]] — all formulas from week 1 topics

## 🗓️ WEEK 2: Speed, Work & Interest (Days 8–14)
*Goal: Master the high-weight "rate" topics*

### Day 8 — Time & Distance
- Read: [[QA_02_Time_Distance]] (full note, 60 min)
- Memorize: km/h ↔ m/s conversion table
- Practice: Problem Bank Q16–27
- Focus: Average speed and meeting time problems
- **Target:** 9/12 correct

### Day 9 — Train Problems
- Read: [[QA_05_Train_Problems]] (full note, 45 min)
- Link to [[QA_02_Time_Distance]] — same formula
- Practice: All train problem types from topic file
- Key focus: Two trains crossing problems
- **Target:** Solve any crossing problem in 90 seconds

### Day 10 — Time & Work (Part 1)
- Read: [[QA_03_Time_Work]] (full note, 60 min)
- Master: The LCM method (eliminates all fractions!)
- Practice: Problem Bank Q28–36 (Easy + Medium)
- **Target:** Solve basic combined work in 60 seconds

### Day 11 — Time & Work (Part 2 + Pipes)
- Review: [[QA_03_Time_Work]] — advanced problems
- Practice: Problem Bank Q37–39 (Hard)
- Pipes & Cisterns: 10 problems
- Connect to: Rate concept in Time & Distance
- **Target:** Solve pipe problems using LCM method

### Day 12 — Simple Interest
- Read: [[QA_11_SI_CI]] (SI portion, 45 min)
- Practice: Problem Bank Q70–75 (SI focus)
- Formulas: SI, time to double/triple, rate from SI
- **Target:** Find any SI value in < 30 seconds

### Day 13 — Compound Interest
- Read: [[QA_11_SI_CI]] (CI portion, 45 min)
- Practice: Problem Bank Q76–79
- Key: CI vs SI difference formulas
- Memorize: Common multipliers — (1.1)²=1.21, (1.1)³=1.331, (1.2)²=1.44
- **Target:** Solve CI problems without calculator in 90 seconds

### Day 14 — Week 2 Revision + Mock
- Full mock: 40 questions (Weeks 1+2 topics)
- Time: 50 minutes
- Analyze: Which topics took longest? Which had most errors?
- Plan: Extra practice for weak topics
- **Target:** 30/40 correct

*Goal: Complete quantitative syllabus*

## ⚡ Daily Routine Template

```
Morning (45 min):
├── 10 min: Formula sheet scan (current week topics)
├── 25 min: New topic reading
└── 10 min: Solve 5 easy problems (build confidence)

Afternoon/Evening (90 min):
├── 50 min: Solve 20 practice problems
├── 20 min: Review mistakes (don't skip this!)
└── 20 min: Re-solve 5 problems you got wrong

Night (15 min):
└── Quick scan of formulas you used today
```

## 🧠 Revision Strategy

### 3-Pass Method for Each Topic
1. **First Pass:** Read full note → understand concepts
2. **Second Pass (1 day later):** Solve all practice questions
3. **Third Pass (3 days later):** Do only hard questions from problem bank

### Spaced Repetition Schedule
- Day 1: Learn topic
- Day 2: Revisit formulas
- Day 7: Solve 5 random problems
- Day 14: Timed practice (1 question = 90 seconds max)
- Day 21: Mock test questions

---

## 🏆 The Final Week Sprint (Days 28–30)

**Day 28:** Weak topics only — spend 3 hours on your 2 worst topics
**Day 29:** Full mixed mock + detailed error analysis
**Day 30:** Rest + light revision + formula sheet only
> Never study a new topic on the last day!

## 🔑 THE Formula (Tattoo This in Your Brain)

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                                          ║
║   Average = Sum of All Items             ║
║             ─────────────────            ║
║             Count of Items               ║
║                                          ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

**Or in short:**
$$\text{Average} = \frac{\text{Sum}}{n}$$

---

## 🔄 The 3 Versions (VERY IMPORTANT!)

From one formula, you get THREE weapons:

| You Want | Formula |
|----------|---------|
| **Average** | Average = Sum ÷ n |
| **Sum** | Sum = Average × n |
| **Count (n)** | n = Sum ÷ Average |

> 💡 **Pro Tip:** In 90% of aptitude problems, you need **Sum = Average × n** more than the average itself!

---

## 📝 Example 1 — Basic

> Marks of 5 students: **40, 50, 60, 70, 80**  
> Find the average.

```
Step 1: Sum = 40+50+60+70+80 = 300
Step 2: n = 5
Step 3: Average = 300 ÷ 5 = 60 ✅
```

---

## 📝 Example 2 — Finding the Sum

> Average age of 4 people is **25**.  
> What is the total of all their ages?

```
Sum = Average × n
Sum = 25 × 4 = 100 ✅
```
*(No need to find individual ages!)*

---

## 📝 Example 3 — Finding Count

> Sum of numbers is **120**, average is **15**.  
> How many numbers are there?

```
n = Sum ÷ Average
n = 120 ÷ 15 = 8 ✅
```

---

## ⚡ Answer to Quick Check from [[01_What_is_Average]]

> Scores: 70, 80, 90
```
Sum = 70+80+90 = 240
n = 3
Average = 240 ÷ 3 = 80 ✅
```

## 🧠 Memory Hook

> **"SANe"** — **S**um = **A**verage × **N**umber  
> Just remember SANe and you have all 3 formulas!

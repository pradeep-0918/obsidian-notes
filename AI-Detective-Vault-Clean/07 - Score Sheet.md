## 📋 Instructions for Judges

1. Score each participant **independently**
2. Score immediately after their 2-minute presentation
3. Use the rubric in [[04 - Judging and Evaluation]] as your guide
4. Do **not** discuss scores with other judges until deliberation
5. Final winner = participant with **highest total across all judges**

> Highest average = 🏆 Winner

---

## 🤝 Tie-Breaking Rule

If two participants have the **same average total**:
1. Compare **Final Verdict** scores only
2. If still tied → judges discuss and cast a deciding vote
3. If still tied → both receive joint first place

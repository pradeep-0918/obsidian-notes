## 🏆 Essential Reading

### Fundamentals
| Book | Author | Why Read It |
|---|---|---|
| Designing Data-Intensive Applications | Martin Kleppmann | The bible. Every DE must read. Explains databases, streaming, consistency at a deep level. |
| The Data Warehouse Toolkit | Ralph Kimball | Dimensional modeling master guide. Still relevant after 20 years. |
| Fundamentals of Data Engineering | Joe Reis & Matt Housley | Modern DE lifecycle, tool choices, career guidance. |
| Snowflake Data Engineering | Joyce Kay Avila | Practical Snowflake guide (from Awesome DE list). |

### Advanced
| Book | Author | Why Read It |
|---|---|---|
| Streaming Systems | Tyler Akidau et al | Deep dive into streaming, watermarks, windows. Written by Google Dataflow creators. |
| Database Internals | Alex Petrov | How databases actually work inside. For senior engineers. |
| Architecting an Apache Iceberg Lakehouse | Various | Modern lakehouse patterns (from Awesome DE list). |
| Learn AI Data Engineering in a Month of Lunches | Various | LLMs + data workflows (from Awesome DE list). |

# 🎓 Courses — Recommended Learning Path

## Free Courses

### SQL
- **Mode Analytics SQL Tutorial** — Best free SQL course, goes from basics to advanced
- **LeetCode SQL** — Practice with real interview questions
- **SQLZoo** — Interactive, beginner-friendly

### Python
- **Python for Everybody (Coursera)** — Free audit
- **Real Python** — Best blog for intermediate Python

### Spark
- **Databricks Academy** — Official Spark training (free tier)
- **Apache Spark Docs** — Comprehensive, official

### Data Engineering
- **DataTalks.Club Data Engineering Zoomcamp** — Free, project-based, covers entire DE lifecycle
- **Seattle Data Guy YouTube** — Practical tutorials
- **Andreas Kretz (learndataengineering.com)** — Structured curriculum

## Paid Courses

| Course | Platform | Cost | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| Data Engineering Nanodegree | Udacity | $$$ | Structured, project-based |
| DE with AWS | Coursera (AWS) | $$ | AWS-specific |
| dbt Fundamentals | dbt Learn | Free | dbt specifically |
| Spark Deep Dive | Databricks Academy | $ | Spark mastery |

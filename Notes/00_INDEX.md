## 🎯 How to Study Smart (Feynman Technique)

The **Feynman Technique** has 4 steps:

```
Step 1 → READ the concept like a story
Step 2 → EXPLAIN it as if teaching a 6-year-old
Step 3 → FIND the gaps (what you don't understand)
Step 4 → SIMPLIFY again — use examples & pictures
```

> 💡 **Golden Rule:** If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it yet!

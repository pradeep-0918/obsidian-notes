## 🧠 Why These 5 Projects?

### For Mr. Cooper
Mr. Cooper is a **mortgage servicing company**. They process millions of home loans.
Their data teams work on:
- Loan performance tracking (who's at risk of defaulting?)
- Customer pipeline data (where is each loan in the servicing cycle?)
- Compliance reporting (regulatory filings, audit logs)
- Market analytics (housing price trends, interest rate impact)

→ **Projects 1, 2, 4** directly mirror this work.

### For JPMorgan Chase
JPMorgan is a **global investment bank + consumer bank**. Their tech teams work on:
- Fraud detection (billions of transactions per day)
- Credit risk scoring (who should get a loan?)
- Algorithmic trading support (sentiment from news/earnings)
- Large-scale data pipelines (Azure, PySpark, Hadoop)

→ **Projects 1, 2, 3, 5** directly mirror this work.

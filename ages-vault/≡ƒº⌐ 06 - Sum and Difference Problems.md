## 🤝 Why Sum + Difference?

> When you know BOTH the sum AND difference of two ages → you can find each age **instantly** without algebra!

This is the **fastest solving method** in all of ages problems.

---

## 🏆 THE GOLDEN FORMULA (Memorize This!)

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════╗
║  Larger  = (Sum + Difference) ÷ 2   ║
║  Smaller = (Sum − Difference) ÷ 2   ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════╝
```

**Why does this work?**
Let Larger = L, Smaller = S
- L + S = Sum ... (1)
- L − S = Diff ... (2)

Add (1)+(2): 2L = Sum + Diff → L = (Sum+Diff)/2
Subtract: 2S = Sum − Diff → S = (Sum−Diff)/2

---

## 🔥 Worked Examples

### Example 1 (Basic):
> Sum of ages of A and B = 44. Difference = 6.

- Larger = (44 + 6)/2 = 50/2 = **25**
- Smaller = (44 − 6)/2 = 38/2 = **19** ✅

⏱️ **3 seconds**

### Example 2 (Find Sum from clues):
> A is 5 years older than B. A's age = 23. Find B.

- Difference = 5, A is larger
- B = A − 5 = **18** (even simpler here!)

### Example 3 (Tricky — sum in future):
> "After 5 years, sum of ages of father and son will be 60. What is their present sum?"

Present sum = Future sum − (5 × number of people)
= 60 − (5 × 2)
= **50** ✅

**Key Insight:** Every year, sum increases by (number of people). So in 5 years for 2 people → sum increases by 10.

## 📊 Sum Problems — All Variations

### Variation 1: Sum increases over time
> "Present sum of A, B, C = 60. What will sum be after 10 years?"

→ New sum = 60 + (10 × 3) = **90** ✅

### Variation 2: Sum in past
> "10 years ago, sum of ages of mother and daughter was 40. Find present sum."

→ Present sum = 40 + (10 × 2) = **60** ✅

### Variation 3: New person joins (Average problem)
> "Average age of 5 students = 14. New student joins, average becomes 13.5"

Total age (5 students) = 5 × 14 = 70
Total age (6 students) = 6 × 13.5 = 81

New student's age = 81 − 70 = **11** ✅

### Variation 4: Person leaves
> "Average age of 10 people = 30. One person leaves, average becomes 29"

Total before = 300
Total after = 9 × 29 = 261
Leaving person's age = 300 − 261 = **39** ✅

## 🧮 Sum of 3 People Problems

**Template:**
> A + B + C = S₁
> A + B = S₂ (then C = S₁ − S₂)
> B + C = S₃ (then A = S₁ − S₃)

**Example:**
> Sum of A, B, C = 75. A + B = 50.
> C = 75 − 50 = **25** ← 1 second!

## 🎯 Difference-Based Problems

### When difference changes? NEVER (see [[📖 01 - What Are Ages Problems]])

### Useful derived facts:
If A − B = D (constant):
- Present: A = B + D
- Past n years: (A−n) − (B−n) = D ← still D!
- Future n years: (A+n) − (B+n) = D ← still D!

---

## 🧠 Sum + Ratio Combo

> Sometimes sum AND ratio are given together:

**Formula:**
```
If ratio = a:b and Sum = S
→ First value  = S × a/(a+b)
→ Second value = S × b/(a+b)
```

**Example:** Ratio 3:7, Sum = 60
- First = 60 × 3/10 = **18**
- Second = 60 × 7/10 = **42** ✅

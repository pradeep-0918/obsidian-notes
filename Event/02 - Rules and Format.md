# 🎯 Rules & Format

> 🔗 Back to [[00 - Home]]

---

## 📌 Competition Rules

1. Each participant receives **one case file** — drawn randomly at the start.
2. Participants must use a **live AI tool** (ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini).
3. They have **15 minutes** to investigate using AI prompts.
4. A **minimum of 4 prompts** must be used during investigation.
5. After investigation, each participant gets **2 minutes** to present their findings live.
6. **No sharing** of case files or prompts between participants.
7. Judges observe both the **prompting process** and the **final presentation**.
8. The participant with the **highest total score** wins.

---

## 🔒 What Is NOT Allowed

- ❌ Asking the AI for the answer in a single prompt
- ❌ Copying a response without understanding it
- ❌ Using pre-written prompts from outside
- ❌ Sharing screens or answers with others

---

## ✅ What IS Encouraged

- ✅ Breaking the problem into logical steps
- ✅ Asking follow-up questions based on AI responses
- ✅ Simulating scenarios to test theories
- ✅ Summarizing findings into a clear verdict

---

## 🎤 Presentation Format (2 Minutes Per Person)

Each participant must cover:

| Part | Content | Time |
|------|---------|------|
| **Problem** | What was their case about? | 20 sec |
| **Prompting Journey** | What prompts did they use and why? | 60 sec |
| **Final Verdict** | What did they conclude? | 40 sec |

## 🔑 Quick Summary

The **AI Detective** event challenges participants to solve a real-world mystery by **interrogating AI step by step** using smart prompts. Each participant receives a case file with a problem. They must craft a series of investigative prompts — not one big question — to uncover clues and build a final verdict.

> 💡 The key skill tested: **prompt engineering** through logical, step-by-step thinking.

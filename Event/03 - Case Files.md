## What Is a Case File?

A case file is a **printed card** given to each participant at the start of the event. It contains:
- A **scenario** (the mystery)
- **Background facts** (incomplete information)
- A **mission statement** (what they need to find out)

---

## 🗂️ Case File #1 — "The Dying Startup"

> **Scenario:**  
> TechNova, a food delivery startup, had 10,000 active users in January. By March, only 200 remained. The founder has no idea why. Revenue has crashed. Investors are panicking.  
>
> **Your Mission:** Investigate the root causes of TechNova's collapse using AI.

### ✅ Sample Prompting Journey

| Step | Prompt Used                                                                           | What AI Reveals                                     |
| ---- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| 1    | *"What are the most common reasons food delivery startups lose users rapidly?"*       | Competition, pricing, app bugs, delivery delays     |
| 2    | *"If delivery delays were the main issue, what user behavior patterns would we see?"* | Negative reviews, low repeat orders, app uninstalls |
| 3    | *"Simulate 5 realistic angry customer reviews for a food app with slow delivery"*     | Vivid complaints revealing pain points              |
| 4    | *"What questions should I ask to determine if pricing was the real issue?"*           | Price comparison framework                          |
| 5    | *"Now summarize all findings into a detective report with the top 3 root causes"*     | Clean, structured final verdict                     |

### 🏁 Sample Verdict
> *TechNova collapsed due to: (1) Delivery delays above 45 mins, (2) No loyalty program to retain users, (3) Competitor launched a 50% discount campaign in February.*

## 🗂️ Case File #2 — "The Failed Fest"

> **Scenario:**  
> Zenith 2024, a college cultural fest, had 5,000 attendees in 2023. In 2024, only 800 showed up. The organizing committee spent more money this year. The HOD is furious.  
>
> **Your Mission:** Find out why attendance collapsed despite higher spending.

### ✅ Sample Prompting Journey

| Step | Prompt Used | What AI Reveals |
|------|------------|----------------|
| 1 | *"What factors typically cause college event attendance to drop sharply?"* | Poor promotion, clashing dates, weak lineup |
| 2 | *"How does poor social media marketing affect event turnout? Give real examples."* | Low reach = low awareness = low turnout |
| 3 | *"Simulate a student's internal monologue when deciding not to attend a college fest"* | Reveals apathy, awareness gaps, peer influence |
| 4 | *"What would post-event survey data look like if the venue was the main problem?"* | Specific patterns to look for |
| 5 | *"Write a 200-word investigation report with 3 causes and 3 recommendations"* | Polished final output |

### 🏁 Sample Verdict
> *Zenith 2024 failed because: (1) Event was promoted only 3 days before, (2) Date clashed with semester exams, (3) No popular guest performers were announced.*

## 🗂️ Case File #3 — "The Crashing Classroom"

> **Scenario:**  
> Prof. Raj's class had 90% attendance in Semester 1. In Semester 2 with the same subject, attendance dropped to 30%. No rule changes. No schedule changes. The HOD wants a report.  
>
> **Your Mission:** Diagnose why students stopped attending Prof. Raj's class.

### ✅ Sample Prompting Journey

| Step | Prompt Used | What AI Reveals |
|------|------------|----------------|
| 1 | *"What are the top psychological reasons students skip a specific professor's class?"* | Boredom, lack of relevance, exam pressure |
| 2 | *"How does teaching style change between semesters affect student engagement?"* | Fatigue, reduced energy, syllabus difficulty |
| 3 | *"Simulate 3 student complaints about a knowledgeable but monotonous professor"* | Realistic student perspectives |
| 4 | *"What attendance data patterns would suggest exam difficulty is the real cause?"* | Drop during specific exam periods |
| 5 | *"Write a formal HOD investigation report with findings and suggestions"* | Professional final output |

### 🏁 Sample Verdict
> *Attendance dropped because: (1) Teaching pace increased sharply in Sem 2, (2) No interactive elements or real-world examples, (3) Students found recorded lectures sufficient as an alternative.*

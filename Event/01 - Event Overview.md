## 🎯 What Is This Event?

**AI Detective** is an individual competition where each participant receives a **mystery case file** containing a real-world problem. They must use an AI tool (like ChatGPT or Claude) to investigate the problem by crafting **a series of smart, step-by-step prompts** — just like a detective interviewing a witness.

The goal is **not** to ask one big question and copy the answer. The goal is to demonstrate **prompt engineering skill** — the ability to break a problem into logical parts and guide the AI toward a meaningful conclusion.

---

## 🧠 Core Concept

Think of it like a detective show:
- 🗂️ The **case file** = the mystery to solve
- 🤖 The **AI** = your informant/witness
- 💬 Your **prompts** = the questions you ask
- 📝 The **final verdict** = your conclusion presented to the judges

---

## 🚫 The Golden Rule

> Participants **cannot** ask the AI directly for the answer in one prompt.  
> They must **build their investigation** through multiple focused prompts.

❌ **Bad approach:** *"Why did TechNova lose all its customers?"*  
✅ **Good approach:** Ask about causes → dig into evidence → simulate scenarios → write the verdict

---

## 👥 Who Participates?

- **Format:** Individual (no teams)
- **Rounds:** Single round
- **Audience:** College students
- **Skill tested:** Prompt engineering, critical thinking, communication

---

## 🏆 What Do They Win?

- A declared winner at the end of the event
- Certificate of participation for all
- Winner certificate + prize

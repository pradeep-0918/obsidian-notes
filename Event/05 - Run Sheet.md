## ⏱️ 1-Hour Event Timeline

| Time | Duration | Activity | Who |
|------|----------|---------|-----|
| 0:00 – 0:10 | 10 min | **Opening & Introduction** | Host |
| 0:10 – 0:12 | 2 min | **Case File Distribution** | Organizers |
| 0:12 – 0:27 | 15 min | **Investigation Round** | Participants |
| 0:27 – 0:55 | 28 min | **Live Showcase Presentations** | Participants + Judges |
| 0:55 – 0:58 | 3 min | **Judge Deliberation** | Judges |
| 0:58 – 1:00 | 2 min | **Winner Announcement + Prize** | Host |

---

## 🎤 Segment Breakdown

### 0:00 – 0:10 | Opening & Introduction
- Welcome participants and audience
- Explain the **AI Detective concept** in 2 minutes
- Show a **live demo** of bad prompt vs good prompt on screen
- Explain rules clearly (see [[02 - Rules and Format]])
- Announce judging criteria (see [[04 - Judging and Evaluation]])

> 💡 Tip: Show a funny/dramatic "bad prompt" example first to get the audience laughing and engaged.

---

### 0:10 – 0:12 | Case File Distribution
- Organizers distribute shuffled case file cards
- Each participant reads their case silently
- No questions allowed after distribution starts
- Participants open their AI tool of choice

> 📎 Case files available here → [[03 - Case Files]]

---

### 0:12 – 0:27 | Investigation Round (15 Minutes)
- Participants work **individually and silently**
- They craft and run prompts on their AI tool
- **Minimum 4 prompts** must be used
- Organizers walk around to ensure no sharing
- A visible **countdown timer** should be on screen

> 💡 Tip: Play light background music during this phase to reduce tension.

---

### 0:27 – 0:55 | Live Showcase Presentations
- Each participant presents for **2 minutes**
- Order: Random draw (or registration order)
- Structure: Problem → Prompting Journey → Final Verdict
- Judges score on [[07 - Score Sheet]] after each presentation
- Host announces each participant's name dramatically

> 💡 Tip: If more than 10 participants, reduce to 1.5 min each to stay in time.

| Participants | Time Needed |
|-------------|------------|
| Up to 8 | 28 min ✅ |
| 9–10 | 20 min (1.5 min each) |
| 11–12 | 18 min (1.5 min each) |

---

### 0:55 – 0:58 | Judge Deliberation
- Judges quickly total scores privately
- Organizer collects score sheets
- Calculate averages (or total) to find winner

---

### 0:58 – 1:00 | Winner Announcement
- Build dramatic tension before revealing
- Announce winner name + score
- Present prize and certificate
- Group photo opportunity
- Thank participants, judges, and audience
